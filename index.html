Trending travel
Dashing through the roads
Pro of driving to your holiday destination: not having to deal with the airport. Con: figuring out where to get a car… and having to actually drive. While we can’t help you with the driving part, we can help you find a rental and ways to save some money. Based on our recent data, here’s what to expect* if you’re renting a ride to your holiday celebrations:
Expect lower prices.
It doesn't matter if you’re staying local or going abroad, rides will cost less than they did last holiday season. When comparing prices year over year, international rentals are 6% cheaper and domestic rental prices are 17% cheaper.
Best domestic rental dates.
For non-New Years holidays, the sooner you drive the better - our numbers show that the cheapest day to start your rental is on 12/19 with a median price of $64 a day. For New Year's road trips, the cheapest day to rent a car is on New Year's Eve.
Best international rental dates.
For those leaving the country to celebrate, the best day to book your rented ride is on 12/23 for a median price of $58 a day. Need a way to get around before then? Car rentals starting between 12/17 and 12/21 will probably cost less than $60 a day.
Bottom line: Drivers get deals.
With many travelers opting to fly this season, interest in driving to holiday destinations has declined. Overall, searches for domestic rentals are down 26% and searches for international rentals are down 8% - resulting in savings for those who choose to drive.
